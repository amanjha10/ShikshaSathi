## Artificial Intelligence (AI) Program Overview
The Bachelor of Science in Artificial Intelligence at KUSOE is a four-year program designed to provide students with a deep understanding of the theory and practice of AI. The curriculum covers core AI topics such as machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and robotics. The program aims to produce graduates who can develop intelligent systems and solve complex problems using AI techniques.

-c-h-u-n-k-h-e-r-e-

## Artificial Intelligence (AI) Program Objectives
- To provide a strong foundation in the principles of artificial intelligence.
- To develop skills in designing and implementing AI models and algorithms.
- To provide knowledge of the mathematical and statistical foundations of AI.
- To foster an understanding of the ethical and social implications of AI.
- To prepare students for careers in the rapidly growing field of AI.
