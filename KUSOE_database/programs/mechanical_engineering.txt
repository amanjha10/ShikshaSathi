## Mechanical Engineering (ME) Program Overview
The Bachelor of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering at KUSOE is a four-year program that provides a broad and rigorous education in the principles of mechanical engineering. The curriculum covers a wide range of subjects, including mechanics, thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, materials science, and design. The program prepares students for careers in various industries, including manufacturing, automotive, aerospace, and energy.

-c-h-u-n-k-h-e-r-e-

## Mechanical Engineering (ME) Program Objectives
- To provide a strong foundation in the fundamental principles of mechanical engineering.
- To develop skills in the design, analysis, and manufacturing of mechanical systems.
- To provide knowledge of energy systems and thermal sciences.
- To foster an understanding of the properties and behavior of materials.
- To encourage innovation and creativity in solving engineering problems.
