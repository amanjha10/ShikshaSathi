## Information Technology (IT) Program Overview
The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at KUSOE is a four-year program that focuses on the application of computer and communication technologies to solve real-world problems. The curriculum covers a broad range of topics, including programming, databases, networking, web technologies, and system administration. The program prepares students for careers in IT support, network administration, software development, and IT management.

-c-h-u-n-k-h-e-r-e-

## Information Technology (IT) Program Objectives
- To provide a strong foundation in the principles of information technology.
- To develop skills in the design, implementation, and management of IT systems.
- To provide knowledge of current and emerging technologies in the IT industry.
- To foster an understanding of the role of IT in business and society.
- To prepare students for a successful career in the dynamic field of IT.
